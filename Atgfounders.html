ATG Impact

ATG Chapel impacts lives through deliverance and healing, community outreach, youth empowerment, faith-based education, and family counseling, fostering spiritual growth, mentorship, and humanitarian support for a transformed society.

Apostle Francis Musili and Bishop Cecilia Musili

Apostle Francis Musili and Bishop Cecilia Musili are the visionary founders of Around The Globe (ATG) Deliverance Ministry, a transformative movement dedicated to spreading the gospel, deliverance, and spiritual empowerment. With its headquarters at ATG Chapel Nairobi, the ministry has grown into a beacon of faith, touching lives across Kenya and beyond.Apostle Musili, a renowned televangelist, Bible expositor, and deliverance minister, has passionately dedicated his life to preaching the message of salvation, healing, and divine breakthrough. His ministry has reached thousands through powerful sermons, deliverance services, and a strong presence on digital platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and YouTube. Bishop Cecilia Musili, his devoted wife and co-founder, has been instrumental in nurturing the vision of ATG Deliverance Ministry. Her steadfast leadership, teachings, and commitment to empowering believers have greatly shaped the ministry’s impact. After years of dedicated service, Apostle Francis Musili and Bishop Cecilia Musili passed the mantle to Bishop Lazarus Musyoka and Reverend Eunice Musyoka, entrusting them to continue the mission of spiritual revival and community transformation at ATG Chapel Machakos. Today, the ministry stands as a testament to their unwavering faith and the power of God’s word.

  • ATG Chapel Nairobi is a dynamic center of worship, healing, and deliverance, impacting thousands of lives across Kenya and beyond.
  • Apostle Francis Musili has led major community outreach programs, offering spiritual guidance, humanitarian aid, and empowerment initiatives.
  • The ministry has transformed lives through deliverance services, mentorship, and faith-based development programs.

ATG Chapel Nairobi serves as the headquarters of Around The Globe (ATG) Deliverance Ministry, located in the heart of Nairobi. Under the leadership of Apostle Francis Musili, the ministry has become a beacon of hope for many, offering a place of worship, healing, and transformation for people from all walks of life.

Through extensive community outreach programs, ATG Deliverance Ministry has touched countless lives. From feeding programs for the less fortunate to youth empowerment initiatives and mentorship programs, Apostle Musili’s vision has always been to uplift society spiritually and socially. His impactful teachings, aired on television and digital platforms, have reached people far beyond Kenya, bringing healing, deliverance, and hope.

ATG Chapel continues to empower individuals through prayer services, leadership training, and charitable missions, ensuring that every believer finds purpose and spiritual renewal. The legacy of faith and transformation continues as the ministry grows, carrying out its divine mission to change lives for the better.
