The Praise and Worship Team at ATG Chapel Machakos is the heartbeat of the church’s spiritual atmosphere. This dedicated group of singers and instrumentalists leads the congregation in heartfelt worship, creating an environment where members can connect deeply with God. Their passion for worship and commitment to excellence inspire the congregation to engage in meaningful praise. Beyond Sunday services, the team also participates in special events, revivals and community outreach programs, making them an integral part of the church’s ministry.
