Bishop Lazarus Musyoka and Reverend Eunice are the visionary leaders of ATG Chapel Machakos. Their unwavering commitment to the church has fostered spiritual growth, unity, and community engagement. With decades of experience in ministry, they provide profound spiritual guidance, nurture leadership among the youth, and spearhead impactful initiatives. Pastors&Deacons;
At ATG Chapel Machakos, Pastors and Deacons work collaboratively to strengthen the spiritual and communal life of the church. Pastors provide spiritual leadership, counseling, and support, while also overseeing the mentee ministry, guiding worship, and teaching. They play a pivotal role in the spiritual growth of the congregation and its engagement with the broader community.Deacons complement his leadership by handling practical tasks such as serving communion, providing pastoral care, organizing church events, and supporting charitable activities. Together, the Pastors and Deacons create a harmonious and effective leadership team, ensuring the spiritual and operational well-being of ATG Chapel Machakos.

Elders&Commitee
Elders at ATG Chapel Machakos provide spiritual leadership, oversee welfare programs, participate in decision-making, and support the pastoral team. Their efforts foster unity within the congregation and ensure the spiritual health of the church. The Church Committee plays a crucial role in managing the logistical and administrative aspects of the church. They organize events, worship services, Bible studies, and community outreach programs. Through their dedication, the committee ensures that the church operates smoothly and serves the congregation effectively. Together, the Elders and the Committee form a cohesive unit that upholds the mission and vision of ATG Chapel Machakos
