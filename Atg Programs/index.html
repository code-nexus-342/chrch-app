Sunday School;
Sunday School Sunday School at ATG Chapel Machakos is a vibrant program that nurtures children’s spiritual growth and creativity. Under the leadership of dedicated teachers, the Melody Dancing Club integrates Bible lessons with creative expression through dance. This initiative fosters teamwork, creativity, and faith among the children. The supportive environment encourages them to embrace their spirituality and develop their talents making the Sunday School program an enriching experience for the young members of the congregation.

Mentee Ministry;
Mentee Ministry The Mentee Ministry is a testament to the transformative power of visionary leadership. Under the guidance of Bishop Lazarus Musyoka, this ministry empowers the youth to become ambassadors of the gospel. It equips them with leadership skills, biblical knowledge, and a deep sense of purpose. Through mentorship and discipleship, the Mentee Ministry plays a vital role in nurturing the next generation of church leaders,ensuring a strong spiritual foundation for the future.

Support Programs;
Support Programs ATG Chapel Machakos is committed to supporting the less fortunate through various initiatives. Under the leadership of Bishop Lazarus and Reverend Eunice, the church has established programs to assist children facing financial challenges through paying their school fees. This includes supporting children’s education, visiting the Destiny Children’s Home, and offering spiritual and material assistance. The Mentee Ministry is actively involved in these efforts demonstrating the church’s dedication to community service and outreach.

Church Program;
Church Programs One of the ongoing initiatives at ATG Chapel Machakos is the construction of a new church building. This project, funded through weekly contributions during Sunday services, aims to create a larger and more accommodating space for worship. Members of the congregation are encouraged to support this noble cause through prayers, donations, and active participation. The new church building represents a collective vision of growth and unity for ATG Chapel Machakos.
